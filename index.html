Privacy Policy for Harmain+

Effective Date: 9 May 2025

Harmain+ ("we", "our", or "us") values your privacy. This policy explains how we handle information when you use our Android app.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personally identifiable information. The app only accesses:

Location Data: Used to calculate Qibla direction and local prayer times.

Internet Access: Used to fetch Islamic content such as Quran Ayahs and prayer data from public APIs.

2. How We Use the Data

Your location is used only within your device to serve features like Qibla and prayer direction. We do not store, track, or share any user data.

3. Third-party Services

Harmain+ may use external APIs (e.g., for Quran and timing info), but no user data is sent to them.

4. Permissions Used

ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION: For Qibla and prayer calculation.

INTERNET: For loading Quran, recitations, and Islamic data.

5. Changes

We may update this policy and will notify users via the app or Play Store listing.

6. Contact

If you have questions, contact us at: info.easy24x7@gmail.com

7. Data Deletion

Users can request deletion or we will delete the data after 90 days automatically without intimation to any users submitted data (such as name and email address provided via the Contact Us form) by emailing us at info.easy24x7@gmail.com. We will process such requests within a reasonable timeframe or delete data within 90 days.
